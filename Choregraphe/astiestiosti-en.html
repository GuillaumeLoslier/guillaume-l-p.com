Asti esti osti

›

Description

A pope and two sisters sit down for a quiet afternoon: crossword puzzles, the Bible, and knitting... But the crossword puzzles aren’t as harmless as one might think! Asti esti osti is a performance where the art of dancing meets the art of swearing. Three performers explore the musicality, power, and humor of this vocabulary that is uniquely ours, turning Quebec swear words into a true rhythmic material. It is not always polite vocabulary, but it is furiously expressive.
